HUMANE TRANSPORT

Our goal is to provide Canadian transporters with an efficient mobile application to support their mandatory transport documentation.

Visit Our GitHub Email Us

Project Background

Canadian livestock transporters have to follow several rules & regulations specified by Canadian Food Inspection Agency(CFIA) every time they are transporting farm animals. Current process requires many printed forms filled by these transporters to make sure that all animals and transporter's data is collected for later track of each transportation.

Our project will focus on creating an interface(a mobile application) which can easily collect all required data and make sure that transporters are aware of all rules & regulations.
