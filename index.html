PROJECT BACKGROUND

Canadian livestock transporters needs to follow several rules & regulations specified by Canadian Food Inspection Agency(CFIA) every time they are transporting farm animals. Current process requires many printed forms filled by these transporters to make sure that all animals and transporter's data is collected for later track of each transportation.

Our project will focus on creating an interface(a mobile application) which can easily collect all required data and make sure that transporter are aware of all rules & regulations.
